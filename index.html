Massachusetts

Massachusetts is a state in New England and the northeastern part of the United States. A state with history and having some of the most culturally significant landmarks, a culture and economy that is thriving, Massachusetts was one of the original thirteen colonies and played a important role in the American Revolution with such significant events like the battle of Lexington and the Boston Tea Party.
